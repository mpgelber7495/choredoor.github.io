Take Your Shoes Off

Take Your Shoes Off (TYSO) is a popular Manhattan based band. They perform a variety of cover music across many decades as well as crisp originals. You can catch them at popular venues across the city such as The Red Lion, Bowery Electric, Session 73, The Bitter End and more. They're not what you would call a normal group of people. Explore the site to find out why... but mainly it's because they're degenerates who light fires in the street.
